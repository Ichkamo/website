42

Created and funded by French billionaire Xavier Niel, the school is a non-profit organization and is entirely free. All the intellectual property belongs to the students. There are currently two official campuses in Paris, France and Fremont, California. The School has been endorsed by many high-profile people in Silicon Valley including Evan Spiegel (CEO Snapchat), Stewart Butterfield (CEO Slack), Brian Chesky (CEO Airbnb), Tony Fadell (CEO of Nest Labs), Jack Dorsey (CEO Twitter), and many more...

Ecole 42

42 is a private, nonprofit and tuition-free computer programming school first opened in Paris in 2013. The school does not have any professors and is open 24/7. The training is inspired by new modern ways to teach which include peer-to-peer pedagogy and project-based learning.
